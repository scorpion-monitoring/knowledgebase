## Overview

Scorpion is a flexible KPI (Key Performance Indicators) monitoring tool designed for project management. It enables service providers to report on project success metrics while maintaining control over their data.

- **Web-based and programmatic access** for KPI submission and retrieval
- **Visualization tools** for KPI monitoring and analysis
- **User and service management** capabilities
- **Flexible framework** applicable across multiple domains

### Benefits

- Improves project management practices through harmonized KPI tracking
- Enhances monitoring efficiency and accuracy
- Supports better impact evaluation and quality assurance
- Enables informed decision-making in project and service management
